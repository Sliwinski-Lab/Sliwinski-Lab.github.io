Some Notes on Auto Updating Publications

So I found a couple of different ways to auto update publications lists for the website, some more automated than others. Probably the simplest option is to set-up an RSS feed and do custom styling, but that has some problems. When I tried creating demo RSS feeds it didn't always list every publication, had publications out of order, and sometimes included publications from other authors. If I play around with it more, I might be able to figure out how to fix some of that.


Another option, which is less automatic, is to use services that let you upload files citation files or use citation managers that contain all your publications and spit out a table or list for the website.
